Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist 
1. Lock Your Social Media: Use the "Private Account" and "Lock Profile" features. This 
prevents strangers from downloading your photos to create morphed AI content. 
2. Think Before You Post: Avoid sharing photos in your college uniform or mentioning 
your specific location (like hostel room numbers) in real-time stories. 
3. Screenshot the Evidence: If you are being bullied, do not delete the messages 
immediately. Take screenshots including the perpetrator's handle, timestamps, and 
the content for legal proof. 
4. The "Stranger Danger" Rule: Do not accept follow requests or messages from 
"Anonymous Confession" accounts. These are often used to bridge into private 
networks. 
5. Use "Report" Buttons: Use the in-app reporting tools on Instagram, X, and 
WhatsApp. Platforms are legally obligated under Indian IT Rules to remove non
consensual sexual content within 24 hours. 
6. Update Privacy Settings: Set your WhatsApp "About," "Profile Photo," and "Status" 
to "My Contacts" only to prevent unknown numbers from identifying you. 
7. UPI Safety (Financial): Never share your UPI PIN for a "Refund." If a cyberbully or 
scammer claims they sent you money by mistake and asks you to "send it back" by 
clicking a link, do not interact.
