Social Media Do’s
● Keep your profile neat and professional
● Always think carefully before uploading any post
● Stay respectful and positive while communicating online
● Protect your personal and sensitive information
● Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts
● Check facts before sharing any news or information
● Communicate politely with others on social platforms
● Give proper credit when using someone else’s content
● Regularly review and update your privacy settings
● Share useful, informative, and meaningful content
● Be aware of who can see your posts and activities
● Use social media as a tool for learning and growth
● Report any harmful or suspicious content immediately
● Maintain a healthy balance between online and offline life
● Follow community guidelines and platform rules

Social Media Don’ts
● Avoid sharing personal details with strangers
● Do not post harmful, abusive, or offensive content
● Never spread rumors or unverified information
● Avoid unnecessary arguments or online conflicts
● Do not overshare your personal life online
● Avoid using simple or easily guessable passwords
● Never use others’ content without permission
● Do not ignore privacy and security settings
● Avoid posting when you are angry or emotional
● Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
● Never engage in bullying or trolling behavior
● Avoid spending excessive time on social media
● Do not upload illegal or inappropriate content
● Do not blindly trust everything you see online
● Avoid posting anything that can damage your image or reputation
